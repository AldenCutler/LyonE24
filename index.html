LYON'S TCL: A MODEL FOR EFFECTIVE TRANSIT SYSTEMS

an investigation of public transit implementation

Hello! We are the Train! team. This webpage serves as a hub for our research on the Lyon public transit system. Here, we display our findings in the form of maps, graphs, and images. If you would like to look at the paper we published, it is available on the WPI website. If you would like to look at the code we used to generate this content, it is available on our GitHub page.
